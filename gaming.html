Table Top Gaming

So starting off as a GM (Game Master) was intimidating. How do I find people to play with? How do I find people I want to play with? It was difficult; because there is a stereotype that everyone that plays table-top games are socially awkward and weird. Well it's kind of true; but that being said they are usually very creative and imaginative. I encourage everyone to at least give it a shot, if not to play at least sit and watch.
